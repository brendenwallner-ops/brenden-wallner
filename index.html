About

I’m Brenden — a developer and designer in Southern California. I founded Pasiflora AI and build products for founders who need something real, fast.

I handle the whole thing: the design, the code, the deploy, and the details most people skip. You work with one person from first call to launch day — no handoffs, no telephone game, no “that’s a different team.” If it ships with my name on it, it works.

Most of my work runs on Next.js and ships to Vercel, but I care more about the outcome than the stack. The goal is always the same: something you’re proud to show off.

Good to
know

How long does a project take?
A marketing site is usually 1–2 weeks. Full apps are scoped after our first call, but I move fast and keep you in the loop the whole way.
What do you need from me to start?
A clear goal and any brand assets you have. No goal yet? That’s what discovery is for — we’ll figure it out together.
Do you do design and development?
Both. That’s the point — one person across design, build, and launch means nothing gets lost in translation.
What happens after launch?
I hand everything over clean and documented, and I’m around for tweaks and ongoing work if you want it.
